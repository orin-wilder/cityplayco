Privacy

What we collect—and why.

Short version: City Play Co. uses your information to run events, send the emails you request, improve the site, and keep guests informed. We do not sell personal information.

Who we are

City Play Co. is a brand of Community Play Tools, LLC. Questions about this notice or your information can be sent to ryan@communityplaytools.com.

Information you choose to provide

  • Email list: your email address when you join the City Play Co. list.
  • Event registration: details such as name, email, phone number, teammate requests, accessibility needs, and other answers you choose to provide.
  • Messages: information included when you contact us.

Website information

The site records limited usage information such as page views, ticket-button clicks, referral source, campaign tags, and a temporary session identifier. Hosting and security providers may also process standard request information such as IP address, browser type, and timestamps.

How we use information

  • to confirm registrations, form teams, support accessibility, and communicate event changes;
  • to send City Play Co. announcements when you have joined the email list;
  • to understand which promotions and site paths are useful; and
  • to protect the site, prevent abuse, and meet legal obligations.

Service providers

City Play Co. currently uses MailerLite for email subscriptions, Google Forms for temporary event registration, Supabase for limited site analytics, and Netlify/Cloudflare for site delivery and security. These providers process information under their own terms and privacy practices.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in an email. You may also ask to access, correct, or delete information by emailing us. Operational event messages may still be necessary after you register for an event.

Retention and security

Information is kept only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes above, recordkeeping, safety, and legal obligations. No online service can guarantee perfect security, but access should be limited to the people and providers who need it.

Age and updates

Current City Play Co. brewery events are for adults 21+. This notice may be updated as the event and its service providers change. The current version is dated July 17, 2026.
