Questions from prospective investigators

The practical briefing.

Is this a scavenger hunt, escape room, or walking tour?

It is a live, hosted detective game with elements of a city scavenger hunt. Downtown St. Pete is the game board: your team follows clues, examines evidence, completes playful challenges, and returns to the brewery to make its case. No escape-room experience or local-history knowledge is required.

Is it genuinely comfortable to come alone?

Yes. Solo guests are central to the design, not an afterthought. The host handles introductions and assigns small teams based on a short intake form, so you won’t be left to insert yourself into an established group.

Do I need to be good at puzzles?

No. The case blends observation, discussion, and deduction. Teams are intentionally balanced, and help is available if you get stuck. The goal is a good shared experience, not a leaderboard.

How does the Pair Ticket work?

It admits two people for $69 during the standard sales period. You’ll provide both names and stay together on one team, while still joining other guests. While Early Tickets remain, two individual $29 tickets cost $58 and are the better offer.

Do I have to drink?

No. Voodoo is the meeting and social venue, but alcohol is never required. Each guest runs their own tab and can order any available nonalcoholic option, food, or drink.

What happens if the weather is bad or too few people register?

The event needs twelve paid attendees. A final go/no-go decision will be made Wednesday, July 29. Tickets are nonrefundable unless the organizer cancels or postpones because of weather or insufficient sales; in that case, paid guests will be contacted and refunded or offered an explicit transfer choice.

Where exactly do we meet?

Voodoo Brewing Co., 220 4th St N, St. Petersburg, FL 33701. The optional pre-game hangout opens at 7 p.m. The briefing and game begin at 8 p.m. Open directions.
