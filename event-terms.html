Event terms & policies

Know the plan before you play.

These policies apply to St. Pete Detective Club: Case No. 001 on Friday, July 31, 2026. If the ticket or registration page shows a more specific policy, review it before paying.

Tickets and confirmation

Submitting the temporary registration form does not reserve a seat. A ticket is confirmed only after payment is received and matched to the registration. Capacity is counted by paid attendees. Pair Tickets use two seats.

Capacity and cancellation

The event is limited to 25 paid guests and needs at least 12 paid attendees to run. The organizer plans to make the final attendance decision by Wednesday, July 29, 2026.

Refunds, postponement, and weather

Tickets are nonrefundable unless City Play Co. cancels or postpones the event because of weather, unsafe conditions, or insufficient sales. In that case, paid guests will be contacted and offered a refund or an explicit transfer choice. If conditions change, operational updates will be sent using the registration contact information.

Walking, weather, and personal responsibility

The experience includes approximately 1–1.5 miles of outdoor walking in downtown St. Petersburg. Guests should wear comfortable shoes, bring water, dress for summer conditions, follow traffic signals and host instructions, stay aware of public surroundings, and avoid unsafe shortcuts. Tell the organizer about accessibility needs as early as possible so reasonable options can be discussed.

Venue, food, and alcohol

Voodoo Brewing Co. is the meeting and social venue. The event is 21+, but drinking alcohol is never required. Food and drinks are purchased separately, and each guest is responsible for their own tab and consumption.

Phones, belongings, and conduct

At least one charged phone is needed per team. Guests remain responsible for their own belongings. Harassment, dangerous conduct, property damage, interference with businesses or pedestrians, and unlawful behavior are not part of the game and may result in removal.

Photography and marketing permission

Photography permission should be requested separately from required event terms. Tell the host at or before check-in if you do not want to be photographed or have your image used in City Play Co. marketing.

Questions

Contact ryan@communityplaytools.com before purchasing if any policy is unclear. These terms were last updated July 17, 2026.
